Welcome to SoundCheck Records

SoundCheck is a record label aimed at focusing on getting things right; investing in the music and talented artists that create it. We believe in creating a collaborative and innovative environment where our artist can thrive and reach their maximum potential in a stress free and serene atmosphere. Our Vision is defined by one Phrase; “Get it right”. We are a team, with a vision, and same goal. We work together as one collective unit, producing all styles of great house music. SoundCheck is an organization that encourages creativity, community, and creates an environment where artists can work on their music freely without distraction or concern.
